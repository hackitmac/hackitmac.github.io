PhaseOne at McMaster attempts to support a wide array of different initiatives all aimed towards making technology easier for students to use and understand. We run weekly code nights aiming towards students who want to collaborate on building software. We also run workshops that aims to improving software literacy, along with speakers to engage and immerse the students within the software engineering community. Our goal here is to eliminate all of the barriers that students perceive about the current technology movement.
