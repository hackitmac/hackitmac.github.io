HackItMac is a student run organization.
We're building a collaborative student tech community at McMaster University.
We welcome students from all faculties with an interest in technology even if you have no experience with coding.

Sign up for our mailing list: http://eepurl.com/2w-8X

Definitions:
Hackathons are collaborative events where you form a team up to 4 and build a product in 24hr using software, hardware and other skills.
Learn more: https://medium.com/@tfogo/hackathons-are-for-beginners-77a9c9c0e000
 

Some of the things we have done this year:
Weekly Code Nights
They’re an open environment for you to come meet new people, learn to code and make things
Buses to Hackathons
We take students to hackathons in the US & Canada. We work to coordinate a bus to take you to schools like Yale, Duke University, McGill completely free.
Workshops
to help students develop industry and practical skills.

DeltaHacks 2015 - Hack For Change

deltaHacks is Canada’s first student hackathon for change. It’s a 24 hr hackathon taking place on the weekend of February 14-15.



The focus of this hackathon is to encourage students to combine programming skills with knowledge from other fields to hack for change.
