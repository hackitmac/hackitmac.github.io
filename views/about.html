PhaseOne is McMaster University’s largest technology community with a reach of over fifteen hundred students. Our team strives to make technology education barrier free and provide extracurricular / hands on experience outside of the classroom. PhaseOne’s mission as a student group is to foster and grow the community by providing the necessary resources.

Promotions

The promotions team for PhaseOne raises awareness of all the events hosted by the team to the McMaster and Hamilton community. We like to engage and alert the community of other nearby tech workshops, highlight projects McMaster students have worked on, and create conversation on technology development and how it relates to us. This is mainly done through social media such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Medium and Snapchat. We are a group of enthusiastic pro-active individuals and are always interested in new members who want to spread the awareness of technology and its benefits.

Maker Space

PhaseOne team dedicated to all things MakerSpace. This includes behind the scenes logistics with operating the space, keeping inventory of and planning what hardware to buy, and being active volunteers in the space. In the near future we will be recruiting MakerSpace executive team members to assist in the planning and design process, as well as general members to help volunteer and man the space.

DeltaHacks

DeltaHacks is McMaster’s annual student hackathon for change. We want to encourage students in all fields of study to come together to build real world applications that create positive change. The ultimate goal behind this hackathon is to include, not only the engineering and computer science students, but all talented individuals from all backgrounds to collaborate, create, and bring ideas to life.
