Upcoming Events

 

IBM Info Session - This Monday

October 19, 2015 || Alumni Memorial Hall, Building #8 || 5:30 PM
Looking for co-op placements or internship opportunities? IBM will be hosting an information session to give you an overview of emerging technologies as well networking. Don't miss this opportunity to chat with the campus recruiters about all the opportunities. P.S. Make sure you register on Oscarplus for attending.
(Register Here)

 

Communitech's "Code to Win" Competition - This Wednesday

October 21, 2015 || IAHS B201 || 5:30-7:30 PM
Communitech is hosting their first event on campus as a competition. It's called Code To Win, and will be a programming challenge open to all McMaster students. You will be required to solve coding problems in your favourite language and ascend the scoreboard for speed and accuracy. The top ranked 50 are invited to Waterloo Region to solve a final written challenge where cash prizes and the opportunity to meet and interview with participating companies are up for grabs. A light dinner will be provided at the exam.
(Register Here)



HackitMac's first Workshop - This Wednesday

October 21, 2015 || TBA || 6:30-8:30 PM
Introducing the Hackitmac workshops. The workshops are going to be an ~8 week series, and will teach you how to be a full-stack developers. This means Web development (front-end, back-end) and ios (maybe, depends on our pace). We'll primarily be focusing on the MEAN stack (mongo, express, angular, and node).
Can't make it? No worries! We will be uploading all of them on our YouTube channel
(Register Here)

 

Toronto Startup Job Fair

October 23, 2015 || Downtown Toronto || 1:00PM - 5:00PM
The Toronto Startup Job Fair connects top talent job seekers with exciting Toronto area startups. This past spring, the Toronto Startup Job Fair has drawn 40+ startups and over 1200 job seekers across Toronto and GTA. Since 2009, we have hosted startup job fairs in SF, NYC, Chicago and Boston. We are back in Toronto for the fall!
(Register Here)

 

Google's Technical Job Interview Workshop

October 28, 2015 || IAHS B201 || 6:00PM - 7:30PM
GOOGLE IS HIRING FROM MAC! They are hosting an event that will focus on their hiring process, how to write technical resumes, and provide some serious interview tips. The Google rep will end with a Q&A session. They are hiring for this coming summer 2016 internships! So don't miss out! To register go on Oscarplus go to -> Events -> Engineering 
(Register Here)

Still haven't applied to YHack? Well, now is your last chance.

Taking place over 36hr from November 6 - 8, 2015 at Yale University

You can register today at yhack.org
Registrations close on Thursday, October 1st, 2015 at Midnight EST!

Afterwards completing the registration:
Make sure to click the confirmation link in the email.

The event is completely FREE!
Transportation to Yale University, food and amenities are provided. This includes a weekend full of learning and fantastic opportunity to interact with big name companies like Microsoft.

What is YHack?

YHack is an international hackathon hosted by and held at Yale, bringing 1500 like-minded hackers and creatives from all over the world.

YHack is a festival of innovation, an arena of tech warriors, and a stage to present your big idea. Jam packed into 36 hours is a rainbow of events (talks, food, rap battles) that go on through the night while caffeinated teams hack on a Python app or program arduino-driven pumpkins to battle over Twitch.

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are weekend-long events where students are encouraged to come with an idea and finish with a product. A more detailed explanation http://bit.ly/1t6gjdx. Dont worry if you dont have any programming experience, hackathons have workshops and mentors to help beginners get started.
