Who's PhaseOne?

PhaseOne is McMaster University’s largest technology community with a reach of over fifteen hundred students. Our team strives to make technology education barrier free and provide extracurricular / hands on experience outside of the classroom. PhaseOne’s mission as a student group is to foster and grow the community by providing the necessary resources.
